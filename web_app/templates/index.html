BITCOIN WALLET GENERATOR


An open-source Python Flask Application to generate bitcoin wallets, multi-sig wallets, and brain phrase wallets






Create a Wallet

  • Simple
  • Multi-Sig
  • Brain Phrase

Simple bitcoin wallets are easy. The simple wallet generates a bitcoin wallet with a seed phrase, private key, public key, and a bitcoin wallet address. A seed phrase allows you to backup your Bitcoin from anywhere. A private key is derived from your seed. A public key is derived from your private key using a SHA256 one-way cryptographic algorithm. A bitcoin wallet is a compressed form of your public key.

Open source project

Contribute to the project on Github! Our goal is to make Flask applications more accessible to the Bitcoin community at large. We wanted to create a simple example for developers of a wallet generator to build off of and play with, while also giving users an easy way to generate wallets. *WARNING* DO NOT USE THESE WALLETS TO SEND MONEY TO. This app is experimental ONLY.
